About Seerah Bot

Seerah Bot is an interactive web companion created to help users learn about the blessed life and teachings of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

Our goal is to make Islamic knowledge, especially the Seerah, easily accessible and engaging for everyone. You can ask questions about the Prophet’s (ﷺ) life, companions, and events, and the bot will respond using authentic sources and historical context.

The information provided by Seerah Bot is primarily based on the highly acclaimed book Sealed Nectar by Safi-ur-Rahman Mubarakpuri, a widely respected scholar who achieved first position for this exceptional work on the Seerah. All responses aim to maintain accuracy, authenticity, and respect for Islamic teachings.

Our Mission

To encourage learning about the Seerah of the Prophet ﷺ through modern, AI-powered conversation tools that combining knowledge, technology, and faith beautifully, while ensuring that all information shared is authentic and reliable.
