About Seerah Bot

Seerah Bot is an interactive web companion created to help users learn about the blessed life and teachings of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ.

Our goal is to make Islamic knowledge , especially the Seerah, easily accessible and engaging for everyone. You can ask questions about the Prophet’s (ﷺ) life, companions, and events, and the bot will respond using authentic sources and historical context.

Our Mission

To encourage learning about the Seerah of the Prophet ﷺ through modern, AI-powered conversation tools — combining knowledge, technology, and faith beautifully.
