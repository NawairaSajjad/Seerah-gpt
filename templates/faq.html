About Seerah Bot

Seerah Bot is inspired by The Sealed Nectar (Ar-Raheeq Al-Makhtum), the award-winning biography of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ written by Shaykh Safiur-Rahman al-Mubarakpuri (رحمه الله). Recognized by the Muslim World League as the best Seerah work, it presents an authentic and detailed account of the Prophet’s ﷺ life, from his noble lineage to his mission and message.
